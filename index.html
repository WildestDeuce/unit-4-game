Crystal Collector!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below; by clicking a crystal a specific amount of points will be added to your score. Win Condition: Match your score to the random number below. Loss Condition: You lose if your score is greater than the random number below. The value for each crystal is hidden until you click on it. At the start of a new game, a new random number is selected and the crystal values change.
